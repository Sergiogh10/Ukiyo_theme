## Style Prompt

A restrained dark-premium product intro for Ukiyo: cinematic, warm, and understated. The title should feel like it emerges from a quiet night sky rather than a tech launch, with soft atmospheric movement, low-contrast texture, and a single warm accent.

## Colors

- Background: `#0B090A` near-black with warm undertone
- Surface depth: `#161A1D` charcoal for soft panels and vignette
- Primary text: `#F4EFE7` warm ivory
- Secondary text: `#B9AFA4` muted stone
- Accent: `#E8B86D` low-saturation gold

## Typography

- Display: `Telma`, expressive serif-like brand title treatment
- Supporting text: `Satoshi`, quiet geometric sans for small caps and subtitle

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use neon cyan, purple-blue gradients, or generic tech glow.
- Do not make the background flat black; keep warm texture and depth.
- Do not over-animate the title; the primary motion is a slow fade and lift.
- Do not crowd the frame with product details; this is an intro, not a feature explainer.
